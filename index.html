About This Project

Health-Tech is a college project built to make health information accessible to everyone. It gives answer for your health questions in simple 1–2 line answers. Features include voice search, text-to-speech, a 3D interactive background, and 100+ built-in health facts. Built with Node.js, Express, and Three.js.
