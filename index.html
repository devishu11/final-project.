About This Project

Health-Tech is a college project built to make health information accessible to everyone. It gives conversational answers to your health questions in simple language. Features include voice search, text-to-speech, Hindi language support, a 3D interactive background, and 100+ built-in health facts. Built with Node.js, Express, and Three.js.
